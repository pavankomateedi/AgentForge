title: ADA 2024 — A1c targets in non-pregnant T2DM
source: ADA Standards of Care 2024 (paraphrased excerpt)
url: https://diabetesjournals.org/care/issue/47/Supplement_1
---
For most non-pregnant adults with type 2 diabetes, a hemoglobin A1c target of <7.0% is reasonable, balancing microvascular benefit against hypoglycemia risk. Less stringent targets (e.g., <8.0%) are appropriate for patients with limited life expectancy, extensive comorbidity, or a history of severe hypoglycemia. More stringent targets (e.g., <6.5%) may be considered for selected patients (short disease duration, long life expectancy) when achievable without significant hypoglycemia.
