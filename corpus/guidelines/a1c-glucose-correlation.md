title: HbA1c interpretation and 3-month average glucose
source: ADA / NGSP (paraphrased)
url: https://www.ngsp.org/A1ceAG.asp
---
HbA1c reflects approximate average glucose over the prior 8–12 weeks; the relationship is roughly: estimated average glucose (mg/dL) ≈ 28.7 × A1c − 46.7. An A1c of 7% corresponds to an average glucose around 154 mg/dL; 8% to about 183 mg/dL; 9% to approximately 212 mg/dL; 10% to roughly 240 mg/dL. Conditions that shorten erythrocyte lifespan (hemolysis, recent blood loss, hemoglobinopathies) can lower A1c artifactually and disconnect it from true glycemia.
