chunk_id: diabetes-foot-screening
title: Annual foot exam in diabetes
source: ADA 2024 (paraphrased)
url: https://diabetesjournals.org/care/issue/47/Supplement_1
---
All adults with diabetes should have a comprehensive foot evaluation at least annually to identify risk factors for ulcers and amputations. Higher-risk patients (loss of protective sensation, prior ulcer or amputation, structural deformity, peripheral arterial disease) require more frequent surveillance. Evaluation includes inspection, monofilament testing, vibration perception, and pulse assessment. Patients with active ulcers warrant urgent referral to a multidisciplinary foot-care team.
