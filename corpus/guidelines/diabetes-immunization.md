title: Recommended vaccinations in adults with diabetes
source: ADA 2024 + ACIP (paraphrased)
url: https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/acip/recommendations.html
---
Routine immunizations recommended for adults with diabetes include annual influenza, pneumococcal (PCV20 alone or PCV15 followed by PPSV23), hepatitis B series for adults aged 19–59 (and ≥60 if risk factors), shingles (RZV) at age ≥50, Tdap once with 10-yearly Td/Tdap boosters, and COVID-19 per current ACIP schedule. Vaccination should not be deferred for chronically elevated glucose levels — diabetes itself is the risk indication.
