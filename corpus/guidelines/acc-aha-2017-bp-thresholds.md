title: ACC/AHA 2017 — Hypertension definitions and treatment thresholds
source: ACC/AHA 2017 Hypertension Guideline (paraphrased)
url: https://www.ahajournals.org/doi/10.1161/HYP.0000000000000065
---
The ACC/AHA 2017 guideline defines stage 1 hypertension as 130–139/80–89 mmHg and stage 2 as ≥140/90 mmHg. For adults with established cardiovascular disease, diabetes, chronic kidney disease, or 10-year ASCVD risk ≥10%, antihypertensive therapy is recommended at a target of <130/80 mmHg. Otherwise, for primary prevention with lower risk, drug therapy is recommended starting at ≥140/90 mmHg with the same <130/80 target once initiated.
